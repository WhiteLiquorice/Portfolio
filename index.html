About Me

This portfolio contains a selection of my writing from this semester in English. The pieces included represent different styles and purposes, including analytical, research-based, and personal writing. Together, they show my development as a writer and my ability to adapt to different assignments and audiences.

Freewriting – Lipogram

This freewriting assignment challenged me to work within strict linguistic constraints. Writing a lipogram forced me to be more conscious of word choice and sentence construction, revealing how limitations can encourage creativity rather than restrict it.

Rhetorical Analysis

In this essay, I analyze how rhetorical strategies are used to persuade a specific audience. The assignment emphasized close reading, structural awareness, and the relationship between language and intent.

Research Essay

This research essay required synthesizing multiple sources into a cohesive argument. I focused on evaluating source credibility and integrating evidence smoothly rather than relying on summary alone.

Reflection Essay

This reflection examines my development as a writer over the semester, addressing challenges I encountered and the strategies I used to improve.

Personal Narrative

The personal narrative allowed me to explore voice and self-reflection in a less formal structure. This piece focuses on clarity, pacing, and emotional honesty.

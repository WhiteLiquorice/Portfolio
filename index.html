About Me

The pieces included represent different styles and purposes, including analytical, research-based, and personal writing. Together, they show my development as a writer and my ability to adapt to different assignments and audiences.

This portfolio is organized as a single body of work, emphasizing progression, argumentation, and revision across multiple genres rather than isolated assignments.

Freewriting – Lipogram

I chose to write a lipogram for my freewriting assignment. The modern revival of lipograms can be attributed to Ernest Vincent Wright and Georges Perec. A lipogram is a work written without using a specific letter, and the omitted letter directly affects the difficulty of the piece.

Constraint-based writing encouraged deliberate word choice and highlighted how limitation can function as a creative tool rather than an obstacle.

Rhetorical Analysis

For my rhetorical analysis, I examined claims surrounding "positive masculinity" and evaluated them through the lenses of ethos, logos, and pathos to assess their persuasive effectiveness.

This analysis focused on how credibility, emotional appeal, and logical structure interact to shape audience reception and belief.

Research Essay

This research essay required synthesizing multiple sources into a cohesive argument. I focused on evaluating source credibility and integrating evidence smoothly rather than relying on summary alone.

Emphasis was placed on synthesis rather than aggregation, prioritizing analytical depth over quantity of sources.

Reflection Essay

This reflection examines my development as a writer over the semester, addressing challenges I encountered and the strategies I used to improve.

Argumentative Writing – Debate Case

This piece represents argumentative writing produced for competitive debate. Although written for oral delivery, the case relies on the same core skills emphasized in academic writing: claim construction, evidence selection, rhetorical strategy, and audience awareness.

The debate format imposed strict constraints on clarity and efficiency, reinforcing the importance of precision in argumentative writing.

Personal Narrative

The personal narrative allowed me to explore voice and self-reflection in a less formal structure. This piece focuses on clarity, pacing, and emotional honesty.
